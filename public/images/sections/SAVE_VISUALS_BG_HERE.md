# Visuals Section Background Image

## Save Your Image Here

Save your visuals background image as:

**File path:** `/public/images/sections/visuals-bg.webp`

## Image Requirements

- **Format:** WebP (recommended) or JPG/PNG
- **Size:** 1920x1080px or larger (will be cropped to fit)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or similar
- **File Name:** `visuals-bg.webp` (or `.jpg`/`.png`)

## Steps

1. **Save the image** you just shared to this location:
   ```
   public/images/sections/visuals-bg.webp
   ```

2. **If your image is JPG/PNG**, you can:
   - Save it as `visuals-bg.jpg` or `visuals-bg.png`
   - Or convert to WebP for better performance

3. **Update the code** if needed (if you use .jpg or .png instead of .webp)

## Image Optimization (Optional)

For best performance, convert to WebP:
- Use online tools like Squoosh.app
- Or use the optimize script: `./scripts/optimize-image.sh your-image.jpg public/images/sections/visuals-bg.webp`
