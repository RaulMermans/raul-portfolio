# 📸 Photography Images - Upload Guide

## 🎯 How to Add Your Photos

### **Option 1: Direct Upload (Easiest)** ✅

1. **Upload your photos directly to these folders:**
   - **Landscape photos** → `/public/images/photography/landscape/`
   - **Architecture photos** → `/public/images/photography/architecture/`

2. **Tell me when you've uploaded them:**
   - Just say: "I uploaded photos to the photography folders"
   - I'll automatically add them to the photography sublanding page

---

### **Option 2: Desktop Drop Zone** (If you prefer)

1. **Create a folder on your Desktop:**
   ```
   Desktop/
   └── Photography Uploads/
       ├── landscape/
       └── architecture/
   ```

2. **Drop your photos in the category folders**

3. **Tell me:**
   - "I added photos to the Photography Uploads folder"
   - I'll move them to the project and update the code

## 📝 Image Requirements

- **Format:** WebP (recommended) or JPG/PNG
- **Naming:** Any name is fine (e.g., `morocco-1.webp`, `building-1.jpg`)
- **Size:** Keep under 1MB per image (I can optimize if needed)
